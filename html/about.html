LET'S PLAY WITH COLORS

COLORS provides an online space to learn and play with colors. Inspired by the layout of It's a shape Christmas, this website features a clean yet colorful design that is both intuitive and interactive.

In GAME, you can find a simple yet challenging color descrimination game that requires players to identify the tile that has the different color within 15 seconds.

In READING, our reading group present the redundancy and modality effects in Richard Mayer's The Cambridge Handbook of Multimedia Learning.

COLORS is designed and built by Yuzhou Wang, a master students at Teachers College, Columbia University, majoring in Instructional Technology and Media. If you want to get in touch with her regarding this website, please email yw2774@tc.columbia.edu.
